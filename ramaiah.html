Ramaiah Institute of Technology

Ramaiah Institute of Technology (RIT) is renowned for its academic excellence and innovation in technology and engineering. Founded in 1962 by the Education Foundation, RIT has continually set high standards for quality education, research, and overall student development. Affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Ramaiah Institute of Technology is dedicated to fostering future leaders in engineering and technology.

Accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA), it is consistently ranked among the top engineering institutions in India. RIT's extensive campus features state-of-the-art infrastructure, top-notch laboratories, and a faculty composed of distinguished academics and industry professionals.

The institute offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in disciplines such as Computer Science, Electronics and Communication, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Information Science, and more.

Admission Process

The admission process at Ramaiah Institute of Technology includes multiple pathways for students seeking enrollment. Various institutional admission categories exist for students who meet specific criteria set by the college.

Recognizing that exceptional talents and individuals with unique abilities may not always meet traditional admission standards, Ramaiah Institute of Technology offers alternative pathways. These institutional admission categories enable deserving candidates to enroll in their desired programs.

Prospective students interested in admissions at RIT should contact the admission office for detailed information on eligibility, criteria, and the application process.

Eligibility Criteria for B.E. Admission

  • Must have completed 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board
  • Mandatory subjects: Physics and Mathematics
  • Optional: Chemistry / Biology / Biotechnology / Computer Science / Electronics
  • Valid score in KCET / COMEDK UGET or equivalent entrance examination
  • Must meet the minimum aggregate score as per VTU and college norms
